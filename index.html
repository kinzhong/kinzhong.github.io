About Me

Hi, there!

My name is Kin Zhong, I'm a Computer Science student studying in Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in Singapore. At NTU, I focused my coursework on researching state-of-the-art fuzzing techniques to find zero-day vulnerabilities in open-source software. During this period, I found 37 security bugs / vulnerabilities in widely used open-source libraries. 8 of them were assigned CVEs, and 3 are pending review. Previously, I studied Digital Forensics in Temasek Polytechnic where I had a lot of hands-on experience learning how to discover and analyse evidence in computer systems. Currently, I'm employed at CSIT as a Security Research Intern.

My career interest lies in cyber security and software development, and I hope to make a positive impact through my work and solve real issues.

I am seeking cyber security related roles in Singapore and would love to connect with you!
